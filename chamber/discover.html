Coffeyville In The Past

  • Coffeyville's peak population was in 1980 with around 18k people.
  • Since then Coffeyville has seen steady declines in population.
  • Coffeyville loses an average of about 1.7% of it's population per year.

The Flood

In 2007 Coffeyville suffered a major environmental disater, in which the town was flooded. The town's refinery had an oil storage tank overflow releasing oil into the flooded water. This disaster has been estimated to have left over 2,000 people homeless, and hundreds of houses and 60 businesses destroyed.

Coffeyville Today

  • Currently Coffeyville has a population of around 8k.
  • Coffeyville's main racial demographic is caucasian with around 72% of residents belonging in that category.
  • 58% of Coffeyville residents are home owners.
  • Coffeyville's Poverty Rate is around 32%.
  • The median household income in Coffeyville is around $54k a year.
  • The Average lifespan of a Coffeyville citizen is a little less than 40 years

Although Coffeyville has been through it's fair share of troubles the people have banded together, new businesses have been blooming and with the combined efforts of the commerce the city can grow and flourish.
