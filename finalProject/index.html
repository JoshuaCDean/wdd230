Based In Carlsbad

Carlsbad is known for it's rich culinary range of foods. We took this to heart to bring you the freshest fruit and produce across many Southern California Coastal regions. With produce locally grown to give you the freshest experience available.

The Coastal Ranges of Southern California are known for the characteristically fertile alluvial soil on the valley floors. Alluvial soil has the highest productivity with respect to other soils, it is one of the best soils requiring the least water due to its high porosity.

Coastal temperatures are fairly consistent, which supports year-round productuon of many row crops. This combined with the incredibly fertile soil make the southern coastal regions of California the perfect place for us to bring you the best produce!
