Overview

Purpose

Our purpose here at Bountiful Foods is to provide easy and large access to the freshest locally grown crops in California! With our website you can easily contact us to get strawberries plucked that morning! Or give us a special order of brocolli which we will set aside for you once in season. Here at Bountiful Foods our goal is to provide easy access to wholesome, filling, fresh foods!

Audience

Bountiful Foods is Targeted towards small local businesses (such as restaurants) who desire fresh produce for their food. The average consumer who are sick of the checmically filled produce of big commerical markets and desire more filling and rich foods, and of course local culinary artists who want to try cooking with the best ingredients harvested locally.
