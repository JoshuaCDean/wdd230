Site Purpose

The Purpose of the Coffeyville Chamber Of Commerce is for local businesses to come together to expand, advertise and make relations with each other. Requests to join the commerce if you are a local business will be accepted. Upcoming Coffeyville events can also be found on the Chamber Of Commerce, as well as the best attractions if you're just in town for a little while.
