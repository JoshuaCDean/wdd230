Alignment

Steam

steampowered.com

This steam page does a good job of aligining it's elements. In the navigation tab all the options are horizontally aligned, giving an orderly look that is pleasing to the eyes.

Repetition

Dell

dell.com

Dell does a good job of using repetition on their page! From just this screenshot you can see they use black lettering for uninteractable text, with white and shades of light gray being the main background color, with blue text representing interactable text. Through their repetitive design it's very pleasing and makes navigation easy.

White Space

Newegg

newegg.com

Newegg does a great job of presenting a lot of information in a way that doesn't clutter the page. This is through the use of white space. The site provides white space between lines of text, and between the image of what you are browsing and the text, making what would be a very cluttered rush of information into orderly and consumable.
